  General & Product Liability Litigation
 

DLH serves as national, regional and local trial counsel for the makers of a variety of professional and consumer products, including chemicals, pesticides, laboratory equipment, HVAC and copy machines. The firm coordinates national product liability litigation involving HVAC equipment, and professional and consumer cleaning products. The attorneys at DLH have represented manufacturers and insurers in a variety of tort cases involving:
• Mold
• Asbestos
• Cleaning supplies
• Latex gloves
• Vinyl chloride
• VOCs
• Pesticides
• Caustics and thorium tailings
• "Sick building" claims
• Multiple defendant, complex litigation
• Auto accidents and other personal injury

In addition, DLH represents clients in general liability and premises liability issues relating to personal injury, breach of contract, and general civil matters. A recent chemical exposure claims was settled for $20,000 just three days after plaintiff made a demand for $600,000, after DLH attorneys cross-examined plaintiff's "expert" at his deposition.

DLH attorneys have appeared or assisted in liability cases in Illinois, and throughout the country, in states including New York, Massachusetts, Maryland, Missouri, Michigan, Wisconsin, Indiana, South Carolina, and California.
